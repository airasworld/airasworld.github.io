AIRA'S WORLD

-

Teaching Computer Science via a Board Game

Aira's World has the mission to teach girls ages 6-10 Computer Science concepts via a collaborative game.

We do so by implementing a clue-based game with deductive logic, to solve ever-changing stories.

We have designed a game that combines robotics, software and board games to encourage collaboration through storytelling.

What does it Teach?

Aira's World focuses on the fundamentals of Computer Science and logical thinking:

- Sequential Thinking
- Spatial Reasoning
- Probalistic Reasoning
- Basic Logic
- Abstraction
- Team work
- Overcoming the fear of Failure

Why?

During the last few decades, women across the world have been alarmingly excluded from the STEM fields.

Previously, more than 30% of the STEM majors graduates were girls. Sadly, in the present, the number of girls pursuing a STEM carreer drop below the 20%.

This is why we created Aria's Word. The first step for solving the gender gap in the STEM fields once for all.

Help us make STEM Education inclusive.

The current choices of toys are flawed. They don't offer a friendly overview of Computational Thinking to kids, and worse, they are too gender biased toward boys, leaving girls on the margin.

However, in order to succeed in our mission, we need you to join our quest. It is only through you young girl, parent , educator, relative, and many others, that we can solve this problem.

Together, let's bring #STEMForAll
